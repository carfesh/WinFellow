contacting us

June 26, 2019

Please check the FAQ that can be found in WinFellow’s User Manual before contacting us, it is usually the fastest way to find a solution to your problem.

There is a WinFellow specific support forum at the English Amiga Board, which is also monitored by us.

You could also make use of the GitHub issue tracker.

Any comments on how to improve WinFellow are welcome!

You can also contact us directly by sending an email composed in English language to Torsten Enderling. Due to copyrights, all emails concerning Kickstart ROM images and ADF floppy images will be ignored.

You can buy Kickstart ROM images from Cloanto.
